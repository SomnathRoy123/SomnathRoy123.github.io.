## Course Overview

This course provides the mathematical framework necessary for advanced physical sciences, focusing on both analytical and numerical techniques.

### Teaching Assistantship Details

In this role, I assisted <b>Prof. Rajesh Kumble Nayak</b> in delivering the course content for level 2 students. My responsibilities included:

- <b>Tutorial Management</b>: Leading sessions to solve complex problems in PDEs and Fourier analysis.
- <b>Numerical Demonstrations</b>: Guiding students through the implementation of Root-finding and ODE solvers.
- <b>Student Mentorship</b>: Providing 1-on-1 support for the probability and statistics modules.

### Prerequisites
- <b>MA1202</b>: Mathematical Methods I
